My name is Vanya Rashev,

I am trying to find the right way to express the world in my head.

Celebrate the human, the marks people make on the world.

Treasure the local, the small-scale, the eccentric, the ordinary: whatever is made out of caring.

Respect what people have built for themselves. Find the beauty in some battered old porch or cluttered, human-scale storefront, while it still stands.
